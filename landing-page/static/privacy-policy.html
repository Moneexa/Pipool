      4. Where the personal information is no longer necessary in relation to the purpose for which it was originally collected/processed. When you withdraw consent. HOW WE USE PERSONAL INFORMATION IN OUR INFLUENCER PLATFORM

  1. What information do we collect and where do we collect it from?

    1. About influencers

      We collect information posted on publicly accessible dot com social media and other publicly accessible websites that are commonly used for self-promotion, marketing and publicity purposes. If information is located behind a paywall or a password or have setting that is other than “public”, we will not collect it. We collect:
      1. Profile picture
      2. Bio information
      3. Social media public profile information:
        1. Facebook- To learn what information is publicly visible on Facebook click the “How is this information shared” section of the Facebook privacy policy here: Privacy Policy.
        2. Instagram- To learn what information is publicly visible on Instagram see the “How is this information shared” section of the Instagram Data Policy.
        3. Twitter- To learn what information is publicly visible on Twitter see the “public information” section of the Twitter Privacy Policy.
        4. Linkedin – We use the Linkedin retargeting pixel as a part of our marketing efforts. Privacy Policy.
      4. The social media handles and public profile information of the followers for each influencer and for a particular post.
      5. The social media handles and public profile information of who the influencer is following.
        1. Location (if this is posted) Email or other contact information (to the extent posted by them on in their name).
        2. Website url (if this is posted).
        3. Sample of posts posted by the influencer together with Text image or video url, number and list of handles of the individuals who reacted (like), commented or shared the post and location data on the post (if this is posted) .
        4. Insights, ideas and thoughts of each influencer.

    2. About Followers

      We collect the social media handles and public profile information (click to the “public profile information” bullet above).

  2. How do we use the information and what for?

    We collect and analyze the personal information we collect about an influencer to create a profile about such influencer to better understand the areas or populations in which they are most influential and the types of audiences to whom they appeal. We collect and analyze the personal information we collect about followers of influencers to help determine areas and populations in which influencers are most influential and the types of audiences to whom they appeal. We process this information for our legitimate interest in providing the platform and related services to our customers and for our customers’ legitimate interest in marketing their products. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect the rights of influencers. For example, influencers can control information we use and share about them as set forth our Privacy Policy, and where our data processing of influencer information is governed by EU data protection laws, influencers also have the rights described in the in the EAA Residents section of our Privacy Policy .

  3. Who do we share it with?

    1. Influencer information:

      We share a profile of the influencer created by our system with our clients in order to enable them to reach out to the influencers who would be most effective for marketing or other campaign that they are considering. The profile contains:
      1. Basic identifying information of influencer from their public profiles
      2. Contact information for the influencer (to the extent listed by them or in their name)
      3. Compiled and aggregated information about particular areas and populations with whom the influencer is most influential

    2. Follower information

      All follower information is shared only in the aggregate. We also share both follower and influencer information as listed in how we share your information- click

  4. How do we maintain the information up-to-date and how long do we keep it?

    In order to facilitate the most effective campaign opportunities for our clients and the influencers we take measures designed to ensure that the information we collect and the analysis we do are accurate and up to date. To this end:
    1. We continuously check each influencer’s source websites and update the profiles on our system.
    2. We apply consistent quality analysis and testing on samples of posts and followers that we use for our analysis.
    3. We employee a team of data scientists that applies algorithms designed to ensure the accuracy of the information.
    4. If an influencer contacts us to correct inaccurate information about them, we assess the request and amend the information where appropriate.

  5. How do we protect the information?

    Our influencer platform database is stored on AWS, which provides robust technical protections for the data. We apply technical and organizational measures to protect this information. For example, we limit access to our database only to specific IP addresses that are under our control and belong to those employees that need to access this information for the performance of their job duties.

  6. What can you do about how we use your information?

    Influencers and followers whose information is included in our platform have choices in connection with how their information is used as explained our Privacy Policy.

  7. What are your rights if you reside in the EU?

    If EU data protection laws apply to you, you are also entitled to the rights listed in the EAA Residents section of our Privacy Policy.

  8. What about information about children?

    The influencers and followers whose information we collect are generally over the age of 18. The websites we use to collect information about influencers and followers are generally limited to individuals over the age of 18. Accordingly, we do not proactively collect or share information about children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information children under the age of 18 without parental or guardian consent we will delete this information. If you have reason to believe that a child under the age of 18 has provided personal information to us, please contact us using the contact information.
